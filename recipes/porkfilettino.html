Pork Filettino

Description

This dish, an Olive Garden restaurant specialty, is traditionally served with roasted potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Pork Tenderloin
  • evoo
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Veal Demi-Glace

Steps

  1. Sprinkle tenderloin with salt and pepper on all sides. Brush with olive oil, rosemary, garlic and parsley. Let marinate approximately two hours. Wipe off marinade.
  2. Cook on a hot grill until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F. Serve with veal demi-glace if desired.
